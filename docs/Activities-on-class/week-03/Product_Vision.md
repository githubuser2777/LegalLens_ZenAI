# Step 04 - Product Vision

# Vision Statement

LegalLens AI helps everyday users understand contracts before signing them by making legal documents easier to review, navigate, and analyze.

The platform reduces the information gap between contract authors and contract signers, enabling users to make more informed decisions without requiring legal expertise.

---

# Product Mission

Our mission is to provide a simple and accessible contract review platform that allows users to:

* Upload contracts quickly
* Understand important terms and obligations
* Identify potential risks
* Verify findings through source citations
* Review contracts with greater confidence

LegalLens AI is designed to assist users, not replace legal professionals.

---

# Problem Statement

Many people sign contracts without fully understanding them.

Common reasons include:

* Contracts are lengthy and difficult to read
* Legal terminology is unfamiliar
* Important clauses are buried within large documents
* Users do not know which sections require attention

As a result, users may unknowingly accept:

* Automatic renewals
* Early termination penalties
* Deposit forfeiture conditions
* Data-sharing permissions
* Restrictive obligations

# Value Proposition

LegalLens AI helps users understand contracts faster and with greater confidence.

Instead of manually reviewing every page, users can:

* Focus on important clauses
* Identify potential risks
* Verify findings through evidence
* Reduce the likelihood of overlooking critical information

---

# Product Principles

## Simplicity First

The product should remain easy to use for non-technical users.

---

## Transparency

Every risk analysis result should be traceable to the original contract content.

---

## Human Control

Users must remain responsible for final decisions.

AI suggestions are informational only.

---

## Privacy Awareness

Only necessary document information should be processed.

User documents must be handled securely.

---

# MVP Goals

The MVP should allow users to:

### Goal 1

Upload a PDF contract.

### Goal 2

View extracted contract content.

### Goal 3

Analyze potential risks within the contract.

### Goal 4

Review supporting evidence and citations.

### Goal 5

Understand important obligations before signing.

---

# Out of Scope

The following features are intentionally excluded from the MVP:

* Legal advice
* Contract drafting
* Court prediction
* Electronic signatures
* Multi-user collaboration
* Enterprise workflow management
* Automated legal decision-making

---

# Success Metrics

The MVP will be considered successful if users can:

### Usability

* Upload a contract successfully
* Complete analysis with minimal guidance

### Efficiency

* Identify important risks within five minutes

### Transparency

* Verify every risk finding using source citations

### User Satisfaction

* Report increased confidence when reviewing contracts

---

# Long-Term Vision

Future versions of LegalLens AI may include:

* Contract comparison
* Multi-language support
* OCR for scanned documents
* Advanced risk scoring
* Contract history and reporting
* Enhanced AI assistance

However, the MVP will remain focused on one core objective:

Helping users understand contracts before signing them.
