# Hoạt động 3 - Product Vision

## Moore Vision Template

FOR

Students, employees, freelancers and consumers who regularly interact with contracts and legal documents.

---

WHO

Struggle to understand legal language, identify hidden risks and make informed decisions before signing agreements.

---

THE PRODUCT

LegalLens AI is an AI-powered contract intelligence platform.

---

THAT

Helps users understand contracts, identify risky clauses, receive simplified explanations and ask questions directly about the document.

---

UNLIKE

Traditional contract reading, generic chatbots or searching legal information manually on the Internet.

---

OUR PRODUCT

Provides explainable contract analysis powered by Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), ensuring that all answers are grounded in the uploaded contract and supported by source citations.

---

# Vision Statement

LegalLens AI aims to become a trusted assistant that helps ordinary people understand contracts within minutes instead of hours.

The platform focuses on clarity, transparency and evidence-based explanations, allowing users to make more informed decisions before signing legal agreements.

---

# Long-Term Vision

Future versions of LegalLens AI may support:

* OCR contracts
* Contract comparison
* Multi-language analysis
* Enterprise compliance review
* Automated audit reports

However, the first version focuses on solving one problem exceptionally well:

Helping users understand contracts before signing them.
